CREOISTA 3.0 is the Third National Level ROBOFEST conducted by the department of Electronics and Instrumentation Engineering of Sri Sairam Engineering College. Thriving over 6 different events Creoista3.0 provides a platform for the young tech fanatics and masterminds to overcome the odds and win exciting cash awards both in technical as well as non-technical aspects.
With “ROBOTICS & AUTOMATION” as its theme, CREOISTA 3.0 is all set to cheerfully welcome the geeky enthusiasts in you.
